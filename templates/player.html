📖 Game Instructions

Goal of the Game

Town: Find and eliminate all Mafia members.

Mafia: Outnumber the Town by eliminating them one by one.

Joker: Get yourself eliminated during a voting round to win!

Character Roles

  • 👺 Mafia: Meets at night to choose a victim. During the day, they must blend in and divert suspicion.
  • 🩺 Doctor: Chooses one person each night to save. If the Mafia attacks that person, they survive.
  • 🔍 Detective: Investigates one person each night to learn their true identity.
  • 🃏 Joker: A chaotic neutral role whose only goal is to be voted out by the group.
  • 👥 Villager: Has no special night powers, but carries the power of the vote during the day.

How to Play

  1. Night Phase: All players "close" their eyes. The GM (Admin) calls each role to perform their action covertly.
  2. Day Phase: The GM announces who (if anyone) was killed. The group then discusses who they suspect.
  3. Voting: The GM opens the voting round. Everyone casts an anonymous vote for the person they want to eliminate.
  4. Elimination: The person with the most votes is eliminated from the game.
💡 Pro Tip: Encourage players to talk and debate! Use private side-discussions and subtle signals to make the game more intense.
